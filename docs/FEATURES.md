# Features

Full feature reference for Paynter Bar Hub.

---

## Navigation

The app is a single page (`pages/index.js`) with a collapsible sidebar. Navigation groups:

| Group | Views |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Dashboard |
| **Stock** | Inventory, Stocktake, Wastage, SOH Reports |
| **Orders** | Order Planner, Purchase Orders, Receiving |
| **Sales & Finance** | Sales Report, FY Chart |
| **Pricing** | Pricing Table, Price History |
| **Bar Operations** | Specials, Notes |
| **Roster** | Volunteer Roster |
| **Administration** | Settings, Audit Log |
| **Help** | Help & FAQ |

Sidebar has 5 selectable themes (persisted to localStorage): Default, Ocean, Forest, Sunset, Monochrome.

---

## Dashboard

- Summary cards: items in stock, low stock count, items on order, current month sales
- Order Wizard banner — 4-step guided ordering workflow
- Quick links to common tasks

---

## Inventory

Live view of all bar stock pulled from Square POS.

**Columns (standard view):** Item, Category, Supplier, On Hand, Serves, Status

**Pricing view** (BMT only, toggle via `$ Pricing` button):
- Buy Price (inc GST), Sell Price, Markup %
- Markup colour-coded: green ≥40%, amber 25–40%, red <25%
- Missing buy price warning (⚠ toolbar badge)

**Controls:**
- Filter by category, supplier, search by name
- Show/hide: rundown items, out-of-stock
- Sort by any column

**Per-item actions:**
- Edit buy price, sell price, units/serves, nip size
- Mark as rundown (excluded from orders and pricing averages)
- View item image (Square catalog)

---

## Order Planner (Reorder)

Weekly reorder calculation based on 90-day sell-through data from Square.

- Calculates weekly average sold (total sold ÷ 13 weeks)
- Recommended order quantity = weeks of cover target × weekly avg − on hand
- Supplier tabs: Dan Murphy's, Coles/Woolworths, ACW
- Editable order quantities
- **Order Wizard**: 4-step guided flow (Review → Confirm → Place → Receive)
- Export to Excel (GemLife_Weekly_Reorder format)
- Save as Purchase Order → generates PO reference, saves to OneDrive

---

## Purchase Orders

List of all open and completed purchase orders.

- PO reference format: `{SupplierCode}-PO-{num}-{date}` (e.g. `DM-PO-001-15May`)
- Status: Draft → Ordered → Received
- View PO details, items, quantities
- Mark as ordered (triggers ref generation)
- Link to receive delivery

---

## Receiving

Process an incoming delivery against a purchase order.

- Opens receive modal with ordered items
- Enter quantities received per item
- Untick items not in this delivery (stay on order)
- **PO Reference field** — visible and editable; pre-filled from PO, amber warning if blank
- Attach supplier invoice PDF → auto-extracted and saved to price history
- Confirm delivery → updates Square inventory, saves documents to OneDrive
- Generates and emails delivery receipt

---

## Stocktake

Manual stock count interface.

- Enter counted quantities per item
- Variance calculation vs Square on-hand
- Accept or reject variances
- Sync accepted counts to Square
- Export stocktake to Excel
- **History**: browse past stocktakes, view item-level history

---

## Wastage

Record stock wastage (breakage, spillage, expired stock).

- Select item, enter quantity and reason
- Syncs quantity adjustment to Square
- Wastage history with date, item, qty, reason

---

## SOH Reports

Stock on Hand reporting.

- **Current SOH**: full stock list with on-hand quantities and values (buy price × qty)
- **SOH Audit**: reconciliation report comparing Square on-hand to last stocktake
- **SOH History**: monthly snapshots (auto-generated by cron on last day of each month)
  - View any past snapshot
  - Compare month-over-month

---

## Sales Report

Sales data from Square POS.

- Filter by date range
- Total revenue, transactions, average transaction
- Sales by category and by item
- Export to Excel

---

## FY Chart

Full-year revenue chart.

- Monthly revenue bars for current financial year (July–June)
- Comparison to prior year
- Cached in Redis, refreshable

---

## Pricing Table

Full pricing view with buy/sell prices and markup calculations.

**Columns:** Item, Category, Supplier, Unit, Serves, Buy Price (inc GST), Sell Price, Markup %

**Pricing policy:**
- Markup target: **40%** (Sell = Buy × 1.40)
- Prices reviewed twice yearly: January 1 and July 1
- No price reductions — only increases proposed
- Average buy price based on last 3 months of invoices

**Price Review modal:**
- Shows items deviating from target by more than BAND%
- Suggested sell prices (rounded to nearest $0.25)
- 3-supplier view with glass and bottle rows
- Margin selector (20–60%)
- Print and CSV export

**Pricing export (Excel):**
- All items with buy price, sell price, markup %, suggested sell
- Glass+bottle wines split into two rows (paired with purple accent)
- Min/Max buy columns (flags wide price spread with red highlight)
- Notes column for anomaly flags
- Overall average markup summary row
- Uses all-time invoice average (730 days)

## Price History

Invoice price tracking. Three sub-tabs:

### Import Invoice
- Upload supplier invoice PDF
- AI extracts line items (Claude Haiku): item name, qty, pack size, unit price
- Review extracted items, correct name mappings
- Save to `buy_price_history` table

### Average Buy Prices
- Shows weighted average buy price per item across invoices
- Date range selector: 30 / 60 / 90 / 180 / All days (default: All)
- Supplier filter
- Update Hub buy prices from averages (bulk or individual)
- **🤖 Auto-match**: AI matches invoice raw names to Hub item names

### Manage History
- All distinct invoice item names with row counts
- Edit Hub name mapping and units-per-pack
- Delete incorrect records
- **🤖 Auto-match button**: sends unmatched items to Claude Haiku for name suggestion
  - Confidence badges: ✓ high (green), ~ medium (amber), ? low (red)
  - Review suggestions, save individually

## Specials

Manage and display bar specials.

- Create specials with name, description, price, image (uploaded to Supabase storage)
- Fullscreen rotating specials display (`/roster/display/specials`) — for bar TV screen
- Active/inactive toggle

---

## Notes

Shared bar operations notes (visible to all bar staff).

- Add, edit, delete notes
- Timestamped

---

## Volunteer Roster

Volunteer session management (merged from paynter-bar-roster).

- Session schedule with volunteer assignments
- Fullscreen roster display (`/roster/display`) — for bar TV screen
- Manage volunteers (name, contact, active/inactive)
- Session history

---

## Settings

App configuration (BMT access only).

- Square location and credentials
- Default markup target (currently 40%)
- OneDrive folder setup
- Bulk actions (e.g. Square inventory sync)
- **Audit Log**: all settings changes with old/new values, timestamp, user
